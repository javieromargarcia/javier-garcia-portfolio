What I Do

I fuse brain dynamics (EEG/fMRI/fNIRS) with physiology (cardiac, respiration, pupillometry, behavior) and neuromodulation to quantify internal state and performance. I emphasize interpretable network biomarkers and closed-loop readiness.

My work bridges the gap between laboratory neuroscience and real-world applications, developing robust biomarkers that can transition from research settings to wearable technologies.

Why This Matters

Humans are flexible, adaptive, and dynamic. Robust foundation models benefit from multimodal priors and causal probes to separate arousal, valence, and control‚Äîenabling better generalization off the lab bench and onto wearable sensors.
